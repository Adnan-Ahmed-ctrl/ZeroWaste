NAMS is the one stop solution for all your international study needs.

NAMS Educational Consultancy provides reliable education consultancy services to students pursuing further education in foreign universities. NAMS is a specialized overseas educational consultancy firm in Ethiopia that connects students with top-ranking educational institutions. The firm was established after market analysis revealed that many education consultants failed to provide the right direction to students seeking higher studies abroad. NAMS helps students make the right choices at the right time for admissions in relevant courses and universities.

NAMS is known for providing expert visa and informational guidance according to high commission requirements and has a strong reputation for securing student visas. NAMS counsels, refers, and processes applications for eligible students for placement in overseas colleges and universities. They respond to Ethiopian students to encourage cross-cultural education.

NAMS Educational Consultancy aims to help students compete globally and become productive members of society. They provide quality education consultancy services and training. NAMS is a fast-growing foreign education consultant. Counselors advise students on educational opportunities in various countries like USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, Germany, France, Sweden, Denmark ,Netherlands, Austria, Finland, Italy, Poland, Czech Republic, Hungary, Switzerland, Spain, Portugal, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, UAE, Turkey, Cyprus, China, Russia, Vietnam and assist with admissions and career choices.
